Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Introduction

Mind Performance Hub ("we," "our," or "us"), operated at mindperformancehub.com, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address: When you voluntarily subscribe through our email capture forms (powered by Kit / ConvertKit). You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Contact form submissions: Name and email when you contact us via hello@mindperformancehub.blog.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, and navigation behaviour, collected via analytics tools.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically by our hosting provider (Hostinger).

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you email updates you have subscribed to (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To respond to enquiries sent to hello@mindperformancehub.blog
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To analyse site traffic and understand how visitors use our content

Email Marketing

We use Kit (ConvertKit) to manage our email list. When you subscribe, your email address is stored securely on Kit's servers. Kit's privacy policy is available at kit.com. You can unsubscribe from any email by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any message we send.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These include essential cookies required for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Hostinger — website hosting
  • Kit (ConvertKit) — email list management
  • ClickBank — affiliate product transactions (we do not process payments directly)
  • Google Analytics / Bing Webmaster Tools — website analytics

Affiliate Links

This website contains affiliate links to third-party products, including products sold through ClickBank. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We do not collect or store any payment information — all transactions are handled by the respective retailer (ClickBank). See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of internet transmission is completely secure. We recommend you do not share sensitive personal information through our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our email list at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@mindperformancehub.blog.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of this website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please email hello@mindperformancehub.blog or visit our contact page.
