⚠️ Before You Start — Important Safety Notes

  • Consult your GP before starting if you have not exercised recently or have a medical condition (NHS guidance)
  • Stop immediately if you experience sharp joint pain, chest tightness, dizziness, or shortness of breath beyond normal exertion
  • Begin at Week 1 regardless of current fitness — progression is gradual by design
  • If you have arthritis or joint pain, keep inclines below 5% until Week 6 and prioritise smooth surfaces
  • Drink water before, during, and after every session
  • Wear supportive walking shoes — replace every 500–800 km or 6–9 months
