## Templates and Patterns from Completed Articles

### Strong Opening Pattern
"[Topic] isn't just about [common misconception]—it's [deeper truth]. [Striking statistic or example]. Yet [common problem in modern life]."

Example: "Sleep deprivation isn't just about feeling tired—it's a systematic assault on nearly every cognitive function your brain performs. After one week of 6-hour sleep, cognitive impairment equals 24 hours of total sleep deprivation. Yet modern culture glorifies 'hustle' and minimal sleep."

### Effective Strategy Format
**Strategy Title: Specific Benefit**
Opening sentence explaining what/why.
- Bullet: specific implementation detail
- Bullet: quantitative guidance
- Bullet: troubleshooting note
Closing sentence with expected outcome.

### FAQ Pattern
Questions should be:
- Actually common (what people really ask)
- Specific enough to answer definitively
- Mix of "how" and "why" questions
- Include contrarian/skeptical questions
Answers: 2-4 sentences, specific, cite research when relevant

### Conclusion Pattern
1. Reframe importance (not optional, foundational)
2. Acknowledge modern obstacles
3. Emphasize quick wins available
4. Specific next action (tonight, tomorrow, this week)
5. Direct call to action with specific first step
