## Writing Approach Established

### Content Structure (Consistent across all articles)
```
├── Introduction (300w) - Hook + scientific context + article promise
├── Why It Matters (600w) - Deep science, mechanisms, specific benefits
├── Core Strategies (800w) - 5-8 detailed, actionable strategies  
├── Common Mistakes (400w) - 4-6 specific mistakes with why/how to avoid
├── FAQ (400w) - 6 substantive Q&As
└── Next Steps (500w) - Implementation timeline + strong conclusion
```

### Writing Style Guidelines
- **Specificity over generality:** "60-67°F" not "cool temperature"
- **Mechanisms over advice:** Explain WHY, not just what
- **Actionable over theoretical:** Implementation focus
- **Conversational authority:** Direct but scientifically grounded
- **No generic content:** Every paragraph topic-specific
